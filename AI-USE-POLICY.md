## Not permitted without consent

a machine learning or AI model on this work or its contents, in whole
or in part, without prior written consent from the copyright holder.
This includes bulk collection or scraping intended to build a training
corpus.

## Permitted

AI assistant checking a claim against the source, a coding agent
reading the repo to answer a question. That is the intended use of a
public, source-linked site.
